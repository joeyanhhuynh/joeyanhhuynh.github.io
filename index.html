Welcome

My name is Joey Anh Huynh; I am a pasionate third year graphic design student from Algonquin College. I started to work as freelancer after first year and I recognized that graphic design is not only my passion, It also helped me discover myself. In fact, I know why I want to be a graphic designer. Because I feel happy when I see people happy with my designs, and when they satisfied with my designs. It means I can help clients to achieve their goals.In addition, graphic design trains my personalities such as It makes me to be more confident and more patient. I take responsibility with each designs and with my clents. It doesn’t matter how much the clients pay for each project, I still try the best I can.

GANGNAM STYLE

I used Lobato Design to create Psy who is very famous with Gangnam Style. I did learn how to design it from Pablo Lobato who born in Trelew, Patagonia Argentina in 1970, is a graphic designer and illustrator. In 1982 he drew his first portrait, an illustration of Cesar Luis Menotti, the coach of the national soccer team at that moment. Everybody seemed to like it, so Pablo decided that this was his thing. But he did not want to be a painter, he admired the artist Uderzo, not Cezanne, as Fine Arts was too much, but graphic design could be a good choice to keep on drawing while paying the bills.

CELLESTIA LOGO

I redesign the logo for Cellestia Cross Culture Marketing. Cross-cultural marketing: Marketing services that will help people present their product across cultures. I used feather to present the meaning of this business. Green is color of trust and confidentiality, tact and diplomacy. Moreover, it attracted people’s eyes.

TEAVANA PACKAGE DESIGN

I design Oolong package of Teavana company which three flavors: Strawberry Rose, Toasted Nut Brulee, and Wild Orangte. My first package is tea box cover; the second package is tea tag. I create Oolong logo by brushing the name on the paper; then I used Adobe Photoshop to fix it. I mix Canadian style and Asian style to create this package. Moreover, I used gold color which make it to be more high class.
